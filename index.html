About

I am an NSF Computing Innovation Fellow and Postdoctoral Associate at MIT CSAIL mentored by Michael Carbin. Previously, I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from The University of Chicago, under the supervision of Hank Hoffmann.

Find me on Google scholar, Tiwtter, Medium.

Research Interests

I’m broadly interested in machine learning, causal inference, computer architecture, and computer systems. My research is about leveraging machine learning and causal inference to better understand modern computing systems and improve system outcomes.
